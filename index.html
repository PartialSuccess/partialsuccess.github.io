Partial Success is a semi-hardcore yet ultra-casual collection of loosely affiliated individuals from among the world's brightest engineers, doctors, carpenters, executives, druglords, and other ne'er-do-wells. We seek to decimate all Classic content within the confines of enjoying our time together first and foremost. The wise and the lovely may find here a suitable home.

More to the point, the guild was founded by a group of twenty-or-so friends who hope to enjoy a bit of a raiding, a bit of player vs. player, and all the rest that warcraft has to offer. We have picked up new friends along the way, not to mention a frankly disturbing number of couples.

Our membership has a disturbing level of experience across all iterations of the game, whether that's the Halls of Naxxramas in vanilla, raiding guilds within the top 250, or private server preparation.

Much of the guild is blue-collar's best or white-collar's most humble. To the extent that we have rules, they are ill-defined below on purpose as empathy goes a long way over exacting laws.

Stipulations, Regulations, etc.

  • Conduct

    • Members will conduct themselves with dignity & respect. Please refrain from language that would be inappropriate in an office, church, in front of your grandparents, or when speaking to a God in front of a bunch of thirty-something gamers.

    Something as crass as discussing politics in guild is simply not done.

  • Loot Distribution

    • Our spin on a Queue system ensures attendance, loot history, and degree of upgrade are taken into consideration.

    Leadership reserves the right to override loot distribution at any time based on performance, attitude, tenure, pre-raid contributions, etc.

    Materials & bind-on-equip items not needed by raiders will go to the Guild Bank.

    Recipes are distributed to our designated crafters at the discretion of Leadership.
