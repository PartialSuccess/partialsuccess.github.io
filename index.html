Stipulations, Regulations, etc.

Partial Success is semi-hardcore ultra-casual generally upsetting guild of top notch engineers, world leaders, and other ne'er-do-wells, and is prepared to decimate all Classic content. Recruiting smart folks who like learning, or whatever.

---

More seriously, we have a core group of about twenty friends or so playing the game, precisely ten of whom are committed to raiding and seeing content. A few of us have seen much of it on private servers already, and raided heavily during the game's first three expansions on retail. We're also loosely associated with another guild on the server that will not be named.

Our members are primarily white collar, put-together types; although, as we mostly hail from midwestern states such as Wisconsin, Illinois, and Texas and have avoided uprooting ourselves to the coasts, you may expect a more blue collar, homespun atmosphere.

In particular, the Guild Master is notoriously unwelcoming of Dunning-Kreuger, Idiot-Yet-Intellectual types. We'd consider it crass to discuss real world politics in our guild description, but that pretty much gives it all away, doesn't it?
