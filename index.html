Partial Success is semi-hardcore ultra-casual loose-fitting guild of the world's best & brightest engineers, leaders, druglords, and other ne'er-do-wells. Our ultimate goal is the decimation of all Classic content, within the confines of enjoying our time together first-and-foremost. If you are wise & lovely, you may find here a suitiable home.

---

More to the point, this guild was founded by a group of about twenty friends, about ten of whom are planning to raid, and all of whom hope to enjoy battlegrounds and other game content. Since our inception, we've picked up some new friends along the way and a frankly disturbing number of couples.

Combined, we have an absurd amount of experience raiding across various iterations of the game: we've seen the inside of Naxxaramas in vanilla, we've led guilds that were in the top 250, and we've enjoyed ourselves as much as possible, especially across the game's first three expansions.

Much of the guild is white-collar, put-together types, with a core that hails from the midwest and is likely to seem blue-collar to some. To the extent that we have rules defined below, they are ill-defined on purpose - you do not need exacting laws when a group of people has empathy for one another. I will add only that we would consider it crass to discuss politics in the guild, which is a revealing enough political statement of its own.

Stipulations, Regulations, etc.

  • Conduct

    • Members will conduct themselves with dignity & respect. Please refrain from language that would be inappropriate in an office, church, in front of your grandparents, or when speaking to a God.

  • Loot Council

    • Loot will be distributed based primarily on attendance, loot history, and degree of upgrade, with secondary considerations given to performance, attitude, tenure, pre-raid contributions, etc. Materials & bind-on-equip items not needed by raiders will go to the Guild Bank. Recipes will be assigned by Loot Council.
