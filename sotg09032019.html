Conduct

To reiterate our conduct policy: "Members will conduct themselves with dignity & respect. Please refrain from language that would be inappropriate in an office, church, in front of your grandparents, or when speaking to a God."

Our first /gkick was Jyp, who engaged in trolling by attempting to create a Deadmines group at level 10, despite repeatedly being told this was not a good idea. This is annoying and disrespectful to others.

Our second /gkick was Woopingu, who committed two sins: first, he crassly brought politics into guild chat in a targeted & insulting way, and secondly, he failed to comprehend that our conduct policy is referencing Ghostbusters. Our guild leadership (mostly) does not believe itself to be the recipient of divine mandates.

Please leave politics out of guild chat. All officers have authority to take disciplinary action as required to enforce this. Being a friend of the GM does not make you immune to this rule.

Additionally, when a recent member left in order to join an RP guild instead, they first posted a kind message before their departure. Unfortunately, when someone joked "Nerd!" this triggered a pile-on which included longer, more unpleasant comments. As a result, Marbella was disgusted and gquit.

First of all - I get the idea, it was a joke. But this really made us look like a bunch of stupid assholes. We should endeavor in the future to maintain at least a bit more semi-professional atmosphere. Please don't respond to earnestly offered kind words with sarcastic mean-spirited humor.

Goals

Our immediate goal is to simply level up and enjoy the game. Classic is back, baby! Level 60 is a long, long ways away and we wouldn't want to spoil the journey too much by worrying overmuch about raiding.

That said, please be on the lookout for other players who you feel would be a good fit for the guild. I would like to keep the guild as small and tight-knit as possible, within the confines of the practical considerations of fielding a 40(!) person raiding roster.

If you are planning to raid, please note that our raid times are currently set for 8-11pm server on Tuesdays and Wednesdays, with Sunday as a backup/optional/flex day. If that time frame does not work for you, now's your chance to get out. If you're in CST like me, that's 7-10pm. I would not expect this window to shift by any more than thirty minutes at most, if at all. It is not inconceivable that we would switch to Tuesday/Thursday, but doing so would require the unanimous agreement of all the Officers and probably most of the people who want to raid.

You can sign up for raiding by clicking here. We will be posting clarification as to exact plans for loot distribution soon.

In the future, you can expect monthly contests, guild events, and especially organized Player vs. Player outings. If you are interested in running or co-ordinating events of this nature, please contact an Officer.
