Fresh Faces

We gained several new members on the evening of Monday the 23rd. The story of these fellows is similar to our own: launch day became a quagmire of queue times and new server openings, and a bunch of folks who had intended to play with one another ended up on different realms. After a bit of shopping around, Daryl and his sister Laurah determined that we would be the best fit for their crew (indeed, Pwent and Xinghua had already made the jump.)

Raiding Roster

Our roster is now in amazing shape, with 28 full-time signups and 11 part-time signups. If you or anyone you know have not joined Discord or signed-up for raiding, please do so. After the month of September, we will no longer be able to guarantee a full-time raiding spot for everyone who signs up.

I apologize for any confusion over the terms full-time and part-time, as these were not adequately explained. The intent is that if you can commit to regularly attending on both Tuesday and Wednesday, you are full-time. In any other situation, such as being able to commit to only one day or being only intermittently available, you are part-time.

Pickup Raiding

We are scheduling a pickup raid group for Sunday the 20th of October, to be planned and executed by us. I am projecting about 15 or 20 members to be raid ready by that time. We will be providing a calendar mechanism for sign-ups in the near future. If you hope to attend, you will need to be at least level 55 and attuned for Molten Core.

We are on the lookout for another guild to partner with for pickup runs, for one or two weeks. Any help in arranging this would be greatly appreciated.

Etiquette

I want to once again encourage everyone to keep guild chat clean & tight. The golden rule is: "Members will conduct themselves with dignity & respect. Please refrain from language that would be inappropriate in an office, church, in front of your grandparents, or when speaking to a God."

If you can express yourself on one line instead of twenty, please do so. If your mother has given you free reign to eat all the candy you want, burn your sugar rush off without spamming guild chat. If you feel compelled to make weiner jokes (weiners being notoriously and understandably humorous by their nature as weiners) make them in party chat. If you are infuriated and catharsis demands you punch the keys for God's sake to create a stream of filth, send it to Algorilla.
